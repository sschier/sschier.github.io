About me

I am a particle physicist transitioning into the world of data science and web application development. I recieved my B.S., Masters, and Ph.D. in Physics at the University of California, Santa Cruz and currently I am a Data Science consultant at TagLeaf, a startup in Santa Cruz, CA

During the last five and a half years of my graduate research, I had the pleasure of working with some amazing physicists from around the world analyzing petabytes (which we systematically reduced to gigabytes)of particle collision data at the Large Hadron Collider (LHC). I worked on the ATLAS Experiment, one of the two general purpose detector experiments at the LHC that confirmed the discovery of the Higgs boson in 2012.
